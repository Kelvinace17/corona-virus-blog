symptoms

  • coughing
  • sneezing
  • sore throat
  • cold
  • difficulty in breathing
  • pain in the body

preventions

  • wash your hands
  • avoid touching eyes
  • avoid touching nose
  • avoid touching mouth
  • use your nose mask
  • sanitize your hands
  • cough or sneeze in a tissue
  • dispose the tissue immediately
